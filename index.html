FAQ.

What is an interactive call?

An interactive call is your opportunity to connect with actors, musicians, artists and athletes over the phone and via live stream. During the LIVE 60-minute event you’ll interact with the host and have the opportunity to ask them your most burning questions. Seriously. You can literally talk with Lorenzo Henrie and ask him anything you want!


How does it work?

It's simple! Sign up for the event you want to join by providing your name, phone number and email. When the event begins we will give you a call at the phone number you provided.


What if I don't have access to a phone?

No problem! You can come back to this page on July 23rd at 4PM PT and watch a live video stream of the event. You'll still be able to ask questions and vote in the special fan feedback segment.


What if I miss the phone call?

We'll leave a voice mail with a call back number to join the event. Or you can come back to this page at 4PM PT on July 23rd and watch a live video stream of the event.


How much does it cost?

Zip. Zero. Nada. It is totally free to participate!

*Message, phone and data rates may apply.
